The whole loop runs on GitHub's servers and a small always-on machine. Your computer can be off. The only human step is the 2-minute approval.

Step A · Research
A

It reads the room — every night.

A scheduled job wakes up and gathers what to talk about: the day's real AI news, your Tech Thursday transcripts, and your weekly steering notes. Always fresh, always relevant, always in your voice.

live AI-news research calendar/topics.md brand voice files show transcripts
Step B · Create
B

It writes and designs.

Copilot writes the caption — a scroll-stopping hook, real value, and a call-to-action. OpenAI paints a cinematic visual to match, and ffmpeg turns stills into video Reels. One high-quality hero per day, on-brand to the pixel.

hook + value + CTA cinematic visuals Reels · carousels · stories tiered hashtags
Step C · Approve
C

You're the final say.

Each morning the engine opens a Pull Request — tomorrow's post, caption, and visuals in one tidy preview. Glance, tweak if you like, tap merge. That's it. Nothing publishes without your approval.

review on GitHub mobile edit anything human approval gate
Step D · Publish
D

Approved → posted.

The moment you merge, the post and its media are handed to Postiz — an always-on publisher — which schedules and pushes it to Instagram at the right time. Feed post, carousel, Reel, or story: each goes out in its native format.

runs 24/7 in the cloud native IG formats smart scheduling
Step E · Measure
E

It keeps score.

Every morning a job pulls your Instagram Insights — reach, saves, follower growth — and matches them to each post. It distills everything into a simple digest: what's winning, what's not, and the best times to post.

reach & saves per post follower delta best format & hours
Step F · Improve
F

It gets smarter daily.

That digest feeds straight back into tomorrow's research step. The engine doubles down on the formats, topics, and timing that grow you — and quietly drops what doesn't. The loop closes, and every day compounds.

closed feedback loop data-driven topics compounding growth
