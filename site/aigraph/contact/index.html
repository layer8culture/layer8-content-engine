Who this is for

Businesses that need to know what AI says before buyers do.

AIGraph is useful when customers are likely to ask AI systems for recommendations, comparisons, local options, service providers, or category advice. The audit shows whether the business appears, whether competitors appear instead, which sources are cited, and whether the answer is accurate.

Best fit

Founder-led businesses, agencies, consultants, local service firms, creator-led brands, and B2B teams with clear competitors and public proof points.
