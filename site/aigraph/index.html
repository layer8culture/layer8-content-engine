The problem

Search is becoming answers, not links.

Buyers now ask AI systems for recommendations, comparisons, summaries, and shortlists. If those systems miss your business, cite weak sources, or recommend competitors, you may never see the lost opportunity in analytics.

The AIGraph question

If a potential customer asks an AI assistant who to trust in your category, what does it say?

How it works

Baseline, improve, monitor.

1. Audit

Run a balanced prompt set across major AI answer engines and compare your business with competitors.

2. Roadmap

Identify the website, content, entity, citation, and trust-signal work that can improve AI visibility.

3. Monitor

Track prompt visibility, competitor share of answer, citation changes, and accuracy issues over time.
