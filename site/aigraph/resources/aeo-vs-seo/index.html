AEO vs SEO

AEO does not replace SEO. It exposes the next layer.

SEO helps buyers find pages. AEO helps AI answer engines understand, cite, and recommend the business behind those pages.

The short version

Traditional SEO focuses on crawlability, rankings, keywords, links, and search traffic. AEO focuses on whether AI answer engines can confidently answer buyer questions with your business included, accurately described, and supported by strong sources.

How they work together

SEO foundation

Fast pages, crawlable content, clear headings, internal links, and indexable URLs.

Entity clarity

Consistent business description, services, proof points, schema, and third-party citations.

AEO monitoring

Prompt tracking, competitor share of answer, citation changes, and answer accuracy.
