AI Visibility Checklist
Resource

Make your business easier for AI systems to understand.

Use this checklist before an AIGraph audit or monthly monitoring setup.

Checklist

  • Confirm the business name, services, location, and audience are clear on the homepage.
  • Add or improve schema for Organization, LocalBusiness, Service, Article, or FAQ where appropriate.
  • Make testimonials, reviews, awards, certifications, and proof points crawlable.
  • Create pages that answer buyer questions, not only brand claims.
  • Make the sitemap and robots file available and accurate.
  • Compare how AI systems describe the business versus competitors.
  • Track which sources AI systems cite.
  • Monitor for inaccurate, outdated, or missing claims monthly.
