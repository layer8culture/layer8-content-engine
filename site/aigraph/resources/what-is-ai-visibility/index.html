AI visibility basics

What is AI visibility?

AI visibility is how accurately and often AI answer engines describe, cite, and recommend your business when buyers ask relevant questions.

The practical definition

AI visibility is not just whether your website ranks in Google. It is whether systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and Google AI results understand what you do, cite reliable sources about you, and mention you when buyers ask for recommendations or comparisons.

Core metrics

  • Brand presence: does AI know your business?
  • Category presence: does AI mention you for unbranded buyer questions?
  • Competitor position: are competitors recommended more often?
  • Citation quality: which sources support the answer?
  • Answer accuracy: is the description correct and current?
