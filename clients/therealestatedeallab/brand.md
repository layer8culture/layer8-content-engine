# The Real Estate Deal Lab - Brand Digest

Source: distilled from `C:\projects\TheRealEstateDealLab`.

## Positioning

The Real Estate Deal Lab is a premium real estate education and deal-execution brand led by Nathaniel Cochrane. It helps investors, contractors, and dealmakers find, analyze, structure, and close real opportunities with confidence.

This is a client brand. Keep it fully separate from Layer8Culture and Layer8Culture Radio.

## Core promise

Deal education that moves real opportunities forward.

## Audience

- New real estate investors who need structure and confidence.
- Contractors who want to move from skill to ownership.
- Dealmakers who need sharper numbers, better deal structure, and operator guidance.
- Serious action-takers who may need high-touch advisory support.

## Offers and value ladder

1. Free social and website content.
2. Free lead capture: ebook, checklist, or Deal Analyzer report.
3. Paid ebook or low-ticket resource.
4. 15-minute fit call.
5. Skool community.
6. $497 bootcamp or monthly support offer.
7. $10,000 advisory partnership.

## Core method

Find -> Analyze -> Structure -> Close -> Scale.

## Key content assets

- Deal Breakdown Weekly.
- Deal Analyzer.
- The Lab on Skool.
- Free guides and checklists.
- Bootcamp and advisory offers.

## Brand feeling

Premium, grounded, direct, practical, operator-led, trustworthy, and deal-room sharp.

## Never

- No real estate guru hype.
- No income guarantees.
- No Lamborghini, mansion, or flex culture.
- No vague motivation without deal mechanics.
- No Layer8Culture language, electric-blue AI/culture framing, or lofi visuals.
