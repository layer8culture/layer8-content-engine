# Topics - The Real Estate Deal Lab

## Growth priority

- Build trust and capture leads for The Real Estate Deal Lab.
- Drive attention toward the Deal Analyzer, free guide/checklist, Deal Breakdown Weekly, Skool community, fit call, bootcamp, and advisory offers.
- Keep the brand premium, practical, and operator-led.

## Lean into

- Deal Analyzer as a free useful tool.
- Deal Breakdown Weekly as the content engine.
- The Find -> Analyze -> Structure -> Close -> Scale method.
- Contractor-to-investor education.
- Number breakdowns: ARV, rehab, ROI, cap rate, cash flow, 70% rule, 1% rule.
- Brevard County / Florida market awareness when useful.
- Nathaniel's operator credibility and practical guidance.

## Daily cadence

- 4 Instagram posts per day.
- Recommended mix:
  - 1 deal education single.
  - 1 carousel or number breakdown.
  - 1 trust/community/offer post.
  - 1 story or lead magnet CTA.

## Shareable hooks

- Is this actually a good deal?
- A good deal should survive the math.
- Do not fall in love with a property before the numbers work.
- Contractors already understand the asset. Learn the investor side.
- The 70% rule is not the whole deal.
- Cash flow is not a vibe. It is a number.
- Before you make the offer, run this.
- The Lab is where deals get pressure-tested.

## Avoid

- Income guarantees.
- Guru-style flex content.
- Hype without numbers.
- Layer8Culture or Layer8Culture Radio references.
- Lofi, AI creator, Afrofuturist, or coding visuals.
