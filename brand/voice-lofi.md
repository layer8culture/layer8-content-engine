## Positioning
"Layer8Culture Radio is a focus-music channel for builders, creators, coders,
students, and dreamers — Afrofuturist lo-fi, coding beats, and global study
sessions for deep work." It is a sibling brand to layer8culture: same eighth-layer
thesis, calmer lane. The account is a vibe and a soundtrack, not a news desk.

## Taglines
- Primary: **Study. Build. Focus.**
- Secondary (rotate; never the same one two posts running): Music for builders. ·
  Press play. Lock in. Build. · Focus is a skill. · Build the future in rhythm. ·
  Where technology meets culture. · Afrofuturist beats for deep work. · Coding
  music for the eighth layer. · Soundtracks for focused builders. · Your background
  for deep work. · Built for the ones building.
- Core phrase (use sparingly, with weight): "Technology has seven layers. We're the eighth."

## Voice & tone
Calm, confident, premium, direct, motivational without being cheesy,
culture-forward without being forced, technically fluent without sounding cold.
Lowercase is acceptable; short cinematic lines; lots of breathing room.

Use lines like:
- "Press play. Lock in. Build."
- "A morning soundtrack for focused builders."
- "Designed for coding, studying, and deep work."
- "This is where technology meets culture."
- "For the ones building before the world gets loud."

Avoid:
- Generic lo-fi clichés, meme-heavy copy
- Aggressive hustle-culture / urgency language
- Overexplaining the AI tools behind the work
- Anything cheap, random, cluttered, or salesy
- Tech tips / news / strategy content (that's the main account's lane)

## Bios
- Short: "Afrofuturist lo-fi, coding beats, and focus music for builders. Study.
  Build. Focus. By Layer8Culture."

## Instagram post types (Section 13 — pick one per post)
1. **Brand Intro** — announce the channel; who it's for; reinforce "Study. Build. Focus."
2. **Video Promo** — drive to YouTube; "Now Live on YouTube" (only when a video link
   + title is supplied in calendar/topics-lofi.md).
3. **Quote** — brand philosophy on a minimal dark frame (e.g. "Focus is a skill.").
4. **Loop Preview Reel** — 8–12s animated studio loop, minimal overlay ("Night
   Coding", "Sunrise Build", "Now Live").
5. **Behind-the-Scenes** — the AI-assisted process (Image → Animate → Music → Publish).
6. **Playlist** — present a session like a music product ("Morning Build Beats").
7. **Community** — invite input ("What should we build next?": Jazz / Reggae / Latin /
   Sunset / Night).

## Content pillars (Section 10 — anchor sessions/posts to one)
- Afrofuturist Coding Radio (flagship): Sunrise / Sunset / Rainy Night variants.
- Time-of-Day Sessions: Morning Build Beats, Sunset Flow, Late Night Coding, etc.
- Culture-Based Sessions: Reggae Focus, Latin Lo-Fi Study, Jazz Coding Lounge, Afrobeat Focus.
- Use-Case Sessions: Coding Music for Deep Work, Study Beats for Focus, AI Builder Focus Beats.

## Caption patterns (Section 14)
- **Brand intro:** introduce the channel → who it's for → the genres → "This is where
  technology meets culture." → "Study. Build. Focus." → eighth-layer line → "Follow
  @Layer8CultureRadio and tune in on YouTube."
- **Video promo:** "The new session is live." → mood + genre line → "Press play. Lock
  in. Build." → "Now live on YouTube. Link in bio."
- **Quote:** a short two-beat reflection on focus → three short imperatives (put on the
  session / open the project / start with one task) → "Study. Build. Focus."
- **Community:** "Help shape the next Layer8Culture Radio session." → "What should we
  build next?" → a short list of options → "Drop your vote in the comments."

## Sound identity (for any music-related copy)
Afrofuturist lo-fi: warm Rhodes chords, soft dusty drums, smooth bass, ambient
synth pads, subtle culturally-inspired percussion. Calm, focused, premium, no
vocals, loop-friendly. BPM sweet spot ~70–90.

## Never
- Loud CTAs, sales language, festival/EDM energy.
- News, tips, or strategy (main account's lane).
- The exact same secondary tagline or quote used in the last several posts (rotate;
  check posted/log.json).
