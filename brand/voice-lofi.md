# Layer8Culture LoFi — Voice Digest (24/7 Afrofuturistic Stream Account)
# Condensed from brand-guidelines-v2.md Sections 6, 9, 18, 20

## Positioning
"Layer8Culture LoFi is a 24/7 Afrofuturistic coding and study stream for
builders, creators, students, and dreamers who need a calm space to focus
and create."

## Tone
Calm, immersive, atmospheric, Afrocentric, futuristic, focused, warm, creative,
reflective. This account is a vibe, not a pitch — Layer8Culture presence lives
in the artwork and stream branding, not sales captions.

## Stream identity
- Titles to use: "Layer8Culture LoFi — Afrofuturistic Beats to Code, Study & Build",
  "24/7 Afrofuturistic LoFi Radio — Beats to Code, Study & Create",
  "Layer8Culture Focus Room — LoFi Beats for Builders"
- Description: "Welcome to the Layer8Culture Focus Room — a 24/7 Afrofuturistic
  lo-fi stream for coding, studying, building, and deep creative work. Settle in,
  open your laptop, and build the future with us."

## Caption patterns
- Scene-setting one-liners + stream link.
- Late-night builder energy: rest, focus, creation as recurring themes.
- Calm and human; lowercase acceptable; no urgency language, no hashtag walls.

## Visual direction (Section 20 — feed these into image prompts)
Center: Black coders and creators, futuristic African city skylines, cinematic
anime-inspired or illustrated studio scenes, soft electric blue glow, warm lamp
lighting, books/plants/code screens/keyboards, West African-inspired figures or
objects, ambient blue and gold lighting, culturally intentional details,
loopable motion potential.

AVOID: copyrighted character references, movie-specific props, Black Panther
masks, direct franchise elements, generic cyberpunk clichés, overcrowded rooms.

## Sound identity (for any music-related copy)
Lofi hip-hop, Afrobeat-inspired percussion, jazz chords, ambient synth pads,
soft vinyl texture, warm bass. Sweet spot 70–82 BPM.

## Never
- Tech tips, news, or strategy content (main account's lane).
- Loud CTAs, salesy language, festival/EDM energy.
