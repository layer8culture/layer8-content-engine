## Core line
**Technology has seven layers. We're the eighth.**

## Voice
Calm confidence. Visionary, intelligent, cinematic, human, builder-first,
culturally aware, direct, reflective, premium, authentic.

## Copywriting rules (Section 10)
1. Lead with belief. ("People should not only consume the AI era. They should help shape it.")
2. Keep sentences cinematic — short lines, line breaks, rhythm.
3. Avoid overexplaining. Let it breathe.
4. Make technology feel human — always connect tools back to people, creativity, culture.
5. Show the work. Tie copy to real builds and public experimentation.

## Hooks & CTAs (growth — every post)
The first line decides whether anyone stops. Open with a HOOK, never a warm-up:
- A bold claim or belief ("Most people will spend the AI era as spectators.")
- Tension / a contrarian truth ("Nobody tells you the hard part isn't the model.")
- A number or list promise ("3 ways we automated our content engine.")
- A real question ("What would you build if the tool could build with you?")
Stay in voice — cinematic and calm, never clickbait, hype, or fake urgency.

Close with ONE clear CTA that earns a growth action, matched to the format:
- Carousel → "Save this." / "Keep this for your next build."
- Reel → "Follow @layer8culture for the build." / "Share this with a builder."
- Story → a poll, a this-or-that, or "reply and tell us."
- Single → a genuine question that invites comments.
Write keyword-rich first lines too (Instagram is searchable): name the tool/topic
plainly so the post surfaces in search. One CTA per post — don't stack asks.

## Tech Thursday tone
Live, direct, educational, builder-focused, accessible, high-signal,
energetic but premium. Positioning: "Tech Thursday is the weekly Layer8Culture
live show where we break down the tools, builds, experiments, and ideas
shaping the AI era."

## Sponsor posts (FOBFLO)
Appreciative, polished, professional, collaborative, founder-driven.
Frame: "The backers. The culture." Sponsors are backers of the culture, not advertisers.
FOBFLO card copy: "CRM • Automation • Client Experience — helping businesses
streamline operations, automate workflows, and scale through modern digital systems."

## Never (Section 9)
Overly corporate, generic startup, gimmicky, meme-heavy, fear-based, robotic,
fake motivational, or excessively technical without context.
Banned phrases: "revolutionary AI solutions", "disrupting the industry",
"next-generation synergy", "unlocking innovation at scale",
"AI-powered transformation platform", "game-changing paradigm shift".
